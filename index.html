MOTTO - Shraddhavan Lavate Gyanam (Knowledge is respect

An English medium, private, unaided co-educational school affiliated to CBSE, run and managed by Hariyana Sewa Sadan Trust ( a charitable Society) which was established in 1992.

OUR MISSION

To provide holistic education in a conducive environment by nurturing the practices of creativity, health and physical education, ethical behaviour and social responsibility leading to lifelong learners with a global outlook ready to face the challenges of the future.

OUR VISION

Every student of Hariyana Vidya Mandir shall be a passionate learner and a global citizen aware of moral values and life skills to face the problems of the future.
